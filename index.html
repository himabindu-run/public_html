About Me

Take charge of your life, with code, chaos and coffee.

Hi! I’m Hima Bindu, a software engineer, founding CTO, and recovering Vim addict. I'm currently building a service-based startup from scratch, writing clean code, mentoring interns, and designing, documenting and architecture solutions for small and mid-sized businesses along with my 70 yr old CEO, Mentor and Teacher... all while juggling a dozen terminal tabs.

My journey into tech started with wide-eyed curiosity (and zero CS background) in my freshman year. Since then, it’s been a rollercoaster of compilers, containers, communities, and caffeine. I’ve worked across Android SDKs, Frontend, backend microservices in Go, dev tools, and infra. And now I’m deep into Elixir and Phoenix, because why not keep things spicy and uncomfortable?

I love solving problems (code or otherwise), leading teams, mentoring juniors, and picking up obscure languages and tools just for the thrill of it. When I’m not coding, I’m either reading books, painting, or tweeting something too philosophical or too stupid.

If you're here after four years - welcome back. The website has changed, but the chaos remains.
